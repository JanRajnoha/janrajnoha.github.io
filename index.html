About

I've worked with computers since I was child. I started with basic webpages, but programming became more interesting. So, I started with Pascal, but in elementary school I tried C#, which is the programming language I still focuse on the most. I developed apps for Windows desktop. Besides these languages, I work(ed) with VB.NET, C++, JS, Java, PHP, HTML/CSS or SQL.

I worked as C# developer at IT Spektrum for 2 years, where I was part of team, who created information system for construction company. Currently I work as junior VB developer at CHAPS Ltd.

After this experience I lectured at Brno University of Technology, Faculty of Information Technology courses Personality Psychology and Communication Skills and became Soft skills lector at Educatoria Institut Ltd. Same year I became project manager in Edukal System, where I started my own education project for university students in Brno.
