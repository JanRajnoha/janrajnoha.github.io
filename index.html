About

I'Ve worked with computers since I was child. After gaming phase, I started creating basic webpages, but programming became more interesting. So, I started with Pascal, which taught me all basic constructs, but I wanted to achieve more, especially see better result than command line program. In elementary school I started with C#, which is the programming language I still focuse on the most. I developed apps on WinForms, WPF and recently on UWP

The biggest project I've created with the use of UWP is an app called The Daily Notes, which is modern organizer and reminder, but due to bachelor’s project I had to stop working on that project and started creating framework for information systems build on UWP, which is included in The Daily Notes in very simple way.

I worked as C# developer at IT Spektrum for 2 years, where I was part of team, who created information system for construction company. After this experience I lectured at Brno University of Technology, Faculty of Information Technology courses Personality Psychology and Communication Skills and became Soft skills lector at Educatoria Institut Ltd, where I started my own education project for students in Brno.

I don’t have much free time, but when I do I listen to music (I don’t like any specific genre) or play on clarinet. I like good movies and series too, mainly comedy or animated.
